    About Us

    Welcome to Radio Prime, Bangladesh’s largest FM Radio Channel. The FM radio channel was launched in 2019 with station broadcasts in Dhaka.The channel became very popular among discarding audience within a very short time.The broadcast station has a dedicated team devoted to programs and News.Radio Prime hopes to demonstrate its commitment to building a strong relationship with the mass people Radio Prime's programs attract the highest listenership.With our fine-tuned programs, you get the best combination of information and entertainment.Besides, the channel’s Facebook page Radio Prime 96.8 FM is the most liked and followed. Radio Prime is committed to putting in place the best possible human in order to achieve excellence in performance. It is a sister concern organisation of Prime Multimedia Ltd. We are always with you.
